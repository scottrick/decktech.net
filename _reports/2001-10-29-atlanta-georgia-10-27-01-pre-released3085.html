I was really pumped when I heard about Reflections III pre-release near me.I printed the directions off MapQuest and off I went. I had a few tense moments on the highway when I thought I had passed the exit, but I didn’t, MapQuest’s directions are just shroud in ambiguity. I got there early, as I always do, to play some pickup games, around 10:30. Guess what? No-one was there! So I drove to McDonalds’ to get a Mr. Pibb, and I went back. By that time, some kids from Savannah were there and I played a quick game with him. (His TR is: http://decktech.net/swccg/treports/reportdetail.php3?deck=3082). He had his Watto deck versus my RST and I lost my Deactivate the Shield Generator right off the top for that bluffing shiznap. I was screwed then, but I only lost by seven. Then more and more showed and I saw Paul Myers and Landon Sommer. Paul and I tried to play a quick game, but Stephen Michael kicked us out of the gaming room because we were about to begin! I paid my twenty bucks and bought four packs of R3 at $3.99 a pack. Significant pulls included Jedi Obi, HOT Leia, EJP Lando Foil, and Queen’s Royal Starship Foil. I had shifted around my work schedule, figuring on starting at 11:30 and playing four rounds, finishing around 4:00. Boy was I wrong! We started at 12:00, drafted and built decks for an hour or so. I was chosen to be light, so I whipped out my LS locations and sat down to draft. I was at a table with the two kids from Savannah, I played one of them earlier (see above), and a really weird collector person. He was pretty freaky! The DS player to my left (his right) opened a pack and Qui Gon Jinn AI Foil was staring back at him. He was pretty mad he had to pass that to me, but I was enthrolled. I did pull two Yoda’s (R3), new Chewie, Falcon combo, and some combat jank. Before we get into the games, here’s my deck:
