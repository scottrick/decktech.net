Damn, what a strange and long weekend, just 2 words before I start to report.

"Watto Decks Just Sucks"

Ok, know I’m ready to start with my first tournament report. The weekend started with the
tournament in Mannheim, it was just a small competition with only 12 players, but 12 players
are enough for Europe, and so we were able to play 6 games, and for me a tournament newbie, it was
just a great expirience, so let’s start with my firts game against :



Christian Wilbs/Watto Deck (it was a deck with the typical winkelhaus style from decktech)

I’ve played Pod Profit and lost the game with -8, but it was a ruf game, and after 20 minutes
fighting for Mos Espa, we started to play our own way, I tried to win the Podrace as fast as possible to
play I Did It, and my "Threepio With .." and "A Gift" kept me alive, cause no drains at Tatooine, and
so I just hoped to get "Honor Of The Jedi" fast into hand to stop the direct damage of 2 every turn, but NO honor for me!
After some hard battles in the Chamber I released finally Han, and then things started to get better, the only thing
that killed me, was my missing Master Luke (He Hasn’t Come Back, Yet). So Maul With double bladed saber gave me the rest,
and the sucking tatooine occupation make my sick. I’ve lost my first game with -8 Damn!!!!



2 Game against a good friend of mine :


Sascha Winter/

Ok, this was just fun, I knew Sascha and his deck, I’ve played over 100 times against him, and I knew his
style to play, so I just wait for his basic mistakes, and my "Court Of The Ville Gangsta and Podrace Deck", was just
to good, the first round i pulled out Gailid, and started to drain for 2 after the first round, after some drains of me
and the podrace with Watto’s Box the game was over, I think it tooked my only 15 minutes to beat him, with +32


3 Game against :

Nico P./Watto Deck

The second Watto Deck in just 3 hours, and that at my first tournament after 1 year, I wasn’t able to beat him in
the chamber, he just deployed Palpy and killed my sweety Threepio and my "A Gift" so I had to try to kill Watto, but
Maul and Vader protected his ass, and after some high drains with mauls stick and the tatooine occupation the game was over
and I’ve lost it with -11 Damn, Watto sucks, and I’ve lost my 3rd game.


4 Game against :

Michael Fritsch/No Objective, No Style,No Way To Kill Me

That was just a beat down, after 5 turns.He played Lars Moisture’s Farm starting and he deployed just Qui without his stick
so with Colo Claw Fish, Jade with stick, and the new Boba, I beat him down, and gave him direct damage of 15 or higher,
after this battle, and after Podracin’ with Watto’s Box, the game was over, and I beat him with +40


5 Game against :

Nico P./Watch Your Step ....

My 2nd Game against Nico, and now I played my ruf Dark Deck, he played a basic Watch Your ... Deck, with Dash and Wedge
in the sky and tons of Palace Raider. But, he wasn’t able to kill my Gailid, and Jade with Stick,Dr.Evazan and IG-88 With Gun
stayed in the Cantina, their were no way for him to beat me, he’s Luke was just killed every time with Colo Claw Fish, and
so I won the race, retrieved 11 Force and win the game with +16


6 Game against :

Andreas Flachsbart/ISB Ops

My last Game, and I hoped to win this game, and I won this game with +26, it wasn’t very hard to beat the sucking ISB
Spy and Outer Rim Scouts, I just finished my Podrace with 5 race dests, and after I canceld his Mobilization Point
with my defensive Shield, he wasn’t able to activate enought force and to beat me. I pull out all my mains to my battlegrounds
fliped my card and gave him a direct damage of 7 or higher every turn, so the game finished after 20 minutes.



Well, this was just a small tournament report of a small tournament in Europe, but I wanted to pull it out to decktech,
a friend of me won the tournament with the new Dark Lightsaber Combat Deck and Dagobah Training Deck (for the light side)
I made the 3rd place with 4 wins and 2 lost games. So it was OK, but WATTO DECKS WILL ALWAYS SUCK!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
