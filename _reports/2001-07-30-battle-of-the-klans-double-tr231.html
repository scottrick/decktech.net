The Battle of the Klans

This is how it works. There are three captains, who
were Steve Brentson, Justin Desai, and Rick Gaudioso
for us. The TD then takes three cardswith different
destiny numbers and each captain picks one. Whoever
got the highest number picks first, and then the
second highest and then the lowest. Then the captains
just keep picking until everyone has been chosen. For
us Desai chose first, then Brentson, then Gaudioso.
Then at the end you add up your total score and
whichever team has the highest wins. I was on
Brentson’s Team. Our Team Consisted of the following
players (In order chosen)
