Saturday was a very big tourney. The first prize was the elusive PS2 and I was determined to get it. I figured my main competition would be the Hunters, Steve baroni, Justin Desai,Steve Brenston and Andy Clema if he made it. I was partly correct because Eric hunter, steve baroni, justin desai, Stev brenston and andy clema all came but a surprise visit from the Terwilleger(sp?) brothers made me think twice about my chances of winning. Hayes Hunter ended up not going which was actually a bad thing since he was someone you could count on to deal out losses to hard opponents. Only about 25 people came which is strange because that is how much we get for the crappy Video Connection tourneys . The prize was a PS2 everyone, not packs of CC! Although there was only 25, the place was loaded with most of our states and regions best players and then some.
I come to the tournament with my same undefeated HD admirals deck and this crappy EBO mains deck for LS. It was really a poor choice because I had never really played it before and because of my lack of experience with it I made some rookie mistakes that cost me big. My brother and I are dropped off at the Hunter&#8217;s house where we get in the car with Mike Pistone and Eric hunter. I am sacrificing doing my homework for the tournament so for the 40 minute ride I just talk with Pistone and get a head start on my homework. Studying Spanish really sucks.
We arrive a little late and I scramble like mad to fill out my decklists and try to find English replacements for my japanese cards. I saw from across the room that Steve Baroni was in a similar situation too. I get finished right before the entry deadline and try to relax. My rating going into the tourney is 2036 with a decent 4-1-1 tournament not averaged in.

Meanwhile, Cody has been playing how did we get into this mess almost every turn but much to my surprise, he is deploying CC battleground locations from hand and not twixes. I try to battle them but he is playing either narrow escape or path of least resistance to get his characters away. With these tactics he gets in a few drains of 3 which hurt a lot. I keep following his charcters with mara, 4lom, ig and others but he just runs away or paths. He is now a little low on paths so I just keep up the tactic while draining for 2 each turn. I then deploy a star destroyer with chiraneau piloting to my carida and start draining for 4 a turn. I also get down battle order but I forget half the time to make him pay for it. I eventually take control of his CC site that lets you cancel drains on CC sites and the game is pretty much over. We battle for it and I lose a few force to orrimarko and company but he is withering away. I block his final drain with mara and the droids and then finish him with another drain of 4. A very sloppy win by 13.
