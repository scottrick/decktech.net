[Pre-tourney]

Alright, it’s Wednesday and I still don’t know what to play. I know for sure some of the people will be running the same crap cause it wins for them, I hardly ever use the same deck twice in a month. So, I’m sitting around my room and thinking "Geez, light side is just @#$%" I mess around with a TIGIH deck, then try the Speederhaulics deck, but that didn’t fit my style. So I say screw it and work on a Dark Side. I fiddle with a Hunt Down speed dueler, and decide on a BHBM beatdown deck. Saturday rolls around and I have about 2 hours to make and playtest a decent Light Side. I’m flipping through my rare binder and decide on Hidden Base X-Wings. Basic stuff, start Squadron A.ssignments, Insight, and Strike Planning. Threw in 14 Wings, Gen Cal. , GS 1, New Wedge and Ship, Tycho and Ship, just for some easy destinies. I toss in 2 Clouds and Wing Cannons for ROps and Zuckuss/Fett hate. Too bad I never got to play a Ral Op deck. On to the tourney

[End Pre-tourney]


Get there around 12 and head over to Burger King to grab some food, eat, and we get started around 1:45 p.m. after Robert Rau (State Champ) got in from Baton Rouge. We had about 11 people I think. On to the games.

Game 2 - My HBX vs. Robert Rau’s [1870’s] ISB

I’m thinking DAMMIT, WHY THE HELL DID I TAKE OUT BLOUNT!! Well, he starts with Imperial Square and drops Yavin 4, so I’m thinking he’s got a freakin huge fleet in his deck. I set up my stuff, Lando in GS1 at Endor 1st turn yadda yadda. I’m pinging him for 1 every turn and he’s building up a m@#$%ive hand. I throw down 4 X-Wings to Endor to back up General and throw down S Foils and Rebel Fleet. He drops Kessel, draws. I’m throwing down system each turn, convert his Kessel. He drops Jabba’s Space Cruiser to Endor and 2 or 3 ORS on it, he then drops ZiMH there and gets hit with It Can Wait. He puts down Devastator, Ozzel, and Evax to Kashyyyk and flips. Moves over to Endor and draws. Hmm. My turn I pull Wedge Leader and throw him to Coruscant with 2 Wings and move em to Death Star and I get away from Endor to Kiffex and flip Hidden Base. Next turn ZiMH comes down with Bossk in Bus to Endor and they move while the Cruiser stays at Endor and he’s chasing Gen Lando. He takes my drains for a while then gets out Den of Thieves. Puts ECC 4 Lom on ZiMH and 2 ORS. So he’s got ZiMH and BiHT. My turn, I put on the foils, drop 3 X wings to ZIMH and BiHT (there were 4 wings there already), drop X Wing Cannons, battle Organized attack, blow Zuckuss out of the sky, draw a 5 for bd and come up with about 40 something and he has to clear Bossk and his loaded Devastator. I drop Legendary Fighter on the Cannon X wing, cause hey, he blew up Zuckuss ;-) Im going around chasing his cruiser and he’s got no more agents on table so hes flipped and I proceed to drain him out.

FW by 17

4(+53)
