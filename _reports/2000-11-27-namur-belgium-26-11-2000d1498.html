Pre-tournament:
I was so disappointed for not going to the Bochum’s tournament last week that I was very happy to make a belgium’s tournament again. I just wanted to play Hunt Down because I haven’t played it for so long. So I have built one and playtested it this saturday: OK Janus is just the man for the duels ! In lightside I chose the same fabulous deck as Jimmy Faelens my friend: it’s a quiet mining colony which retrieve a lot (on/off the edge and tracked perfect sabacc). This deck is a tracking machine :) So I went with some friends to Namur in BELGIUM. 24 players showed up for this tournament so it was very good...

Game#1 Dark against Patrick Stevens (Profit)
I started Crush the rebellion (to avoid clash of saber) in addition of the traditional IAO/Mobilization Point. I added Mara Jade and Dr. Evazan in Audience Chamber. I pulled off Rendili and executor docking bay for activation. Since I can’t afford deploying Lord Vader I deployed mara’s saber from reserve deck. We lost from Visage and he took his turn: Ben Kenobi + his saber + master luke + Kal faln c’ndros against Mara + Dr. Evazan. He tried to send evazan in my hand but failed. I hit Kal and she is emmediatly operated by the evil doctor. Ben Kenobi suceeded to hit Mara and after battle destinies, my 2 characters were dead (I lost 3 more forces) and Ben just revived Luke. OK it was a bad start since he released han. At that time he was draining of 2 and I was losing 1 from profit and 1 a turn from my own Visage. I just put Janus on executor for activation and to plan some tracked destinies. I drew a lot and played Monnok (he had no card against duel)... I then put Lord Vader & Saber + Tarkin, initiated Vader’s Obsession and drew my tracked masterful move + visage. Of course I won the duel , he gave me Luke and lost 5. I initiated and hit Ben. He failed to hit Vader (I think he wanted I lost like him from Visage), I canceled his destiny and Han took the attrition (he flipped back)... I then moved at Jabba’s Palace to drain for 3. I put Boba in Slave 1 at Rendili. He lost from my two drains and visage and drew a lot. He put Lando with vibro axe on holotheatre and canceled my visage. I took it back with no escape, put IG-88 in front of Lando and initiated. I captured it... Without Luke and without lando he couldn’t stand in front of Judge Dred (Lord Vader + Saber) and Tarkin. I used Masterfull move like a reactor terminal with Janus (replace a card and draw your masterful move, play it with no hologram in the deck and redraw it each turn). My drains and visage stopped the game fastly...

Game#2 Light against Jean Philippe Buchkremer (Ralltiir Ops)
When I saw his objective, I felt very bad. I couldn’t drain if I didn’t flip (it’s hard to hold on Bespin system against Ralltiir). Like all the Ralltiir ops, he set a big activation with Carida and Mobilization Point. But Jean Philippe was playing Biker Scouts and Speeder Bikes instead of At-Sts ! Since I was playing Patrol Craft and Palace Raiders, I feared High Speed Tactics :( At the end of his second turn, Ralltiir Ops was flipped. I have just put all my sites and drawn a lot of cards. I drew a card which will perhaps help me: Surprise Assault. My strategy was to track big destinies to play it and then retrieve it. But the first card I lost from my deck was Off The Edge. So he immediatly pulled off Something Special Planned For Them :( I put a pile of characters on Cloud City (including Patrol Craft and Palace Raiders) to avoid a lost Monnok. I then recycled some 6’s and he should have thought it was for On/Off The edge. So he drained on a site where 5 cards where present (all unique Biker Scouts and Speeder Bikes with a total power of 10). I played Surprise Assault with a tracked 6/6/5’s destinies. A 4’s and a 3’s have been added and he had to lost 14
14 forces :) Then I transited on Ralltiir to avoid one drain and to force him to play an early High Speed Tactic (I had a stack in hand to grab it). My plan worked and he played one with his lone Speeder Bike. I grabbed it and lost my Patrol Craft. Since his force pile and his hand was small (because he has deployed and lost a lot of cards) he moved away is Biker. He stopped draining by fearing another Surprise Assault. He didn’t come on Cloud City due to my lot of battle destinies (Palace Raider, Lando with Blaster, Han EPP and Luke EPP). Then I played an Off The Edge and an On The Edge (they were placed out of play) to retrieve 12 forces. I came back on Cloud City, added Patrol Craft and good aliens (Raiders and Taws Khaa or perhaps Melas I don’t remember...), spread out and flipped. He read my objective and started his turn: Zuckuss came on Bespin and flipped it back. He went on Ralltiir to avoid a beatdown (I had retrieve a lot of cards and I could activate a lot). I played Tunnel Vision and took Home One. I put it with Hash’n and Thaneespi then added I’ll Take The Leader: the Super Home One was now complete ! I put the Celebration and moved a patrol craft on Cloud City sector and flipped again. He organised his troops to drain safely (with just Lord Vader for example). But at that time my drain of 7 (1 on bespin, 1 on cloud city, 1 on casino, 2 on dowtown plazza and 2 on the docking bay with the admiral order) couldn’t be canceled or modified ! My celebration retrieved all my lost pile and I won without cards in it :)
