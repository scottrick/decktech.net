DeckTech Community Communication Standards
DeckTech's Online Forums consist of message boards, chat rooms, editoral columns, and other dynamic content areas provided by DeckTech. DeckTech's Online Forums are available for open discussion and free exchange of ideas. The postings are not edited for content, style or errors in mechanics of fact.

Like the community you live in, the DeckTech Online Forums work best when everyone follows basic standards of conduct. Your privilege to use and contribute to discussions depends on your compliance with the following guidelines. DeckTech may revoke your registration privileges and/or take any other appropriate measures to enforce these guidelines if violations are brought to our attention.

Online Forum Guidelines
As an DeckTech Member you are entirely responsible and liable for all activities conducted through your Account. Listed below are some, though not all, violations that may result in DeckTech terminating your Account. By registering an DeckTech member name, you agree to not engage in any of the following acts:

  • Do not harass, threaten, embarrass or cause distress or discomfort upon other DeckTech online forum participants or other individual or group.
  • Do not transmit via DeckTech Online Forums any information, data, text, files, links, software, chat, communication or other materials ("Content") that is unlawful: harmful: threatening: abusive: harassing: defamatory: vulgar: obscene; or racially or ethnically hateful including that pertaining to sexual orientation.
  • Do not impersonate in DeckTech Online Forums any person, particularly an DeckTech official, or DeckTech Host.
  • Do not post or transmit any unsolicited advertising, promotional materials, or any other forms of solicitation in DeckTech Online Forums, except in those areas that are designated for such a purpose.
  • Do not intentionally or unintentionally violate any applicable local, state, national or international law, including but not limited to any regulations having the force of law while using or accessing DeckTech Online Forums or in connection with your use of DeckTech Online Forums in any manner.
DeckTech reserves the right to monitor some, all, or no areas of DeckTech Online Forums for adherence to these Rules or other rules that may be published from time to time. You acknowledge that by providing you with the ability to distribute your content, DeckTech is acting as a passive conduit for such distribution and DeckTech is not undertaking any obligation or liability relating to any content or activities in a topic area.

DeckTech is not undertaking any obligation or liability to remove requested content in a timely fashion or at all.
