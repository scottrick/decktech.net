DeckTech Rulings Minder Help

Back to Rulings Minder

Thank you for using the DeckTech Rulings Minder. There are several features that make our current rulings system unique and will enable you to spend more time doing what you like to do: building decks and playing. First let us look at some of the basic features.

The Basics

Every ruling has three parts, title, ruling and it's confirmation source.


Title: This is the card or game action the ruling addresses.
Ruling: This is the actual ruling about the card or game action that was made.
Confirmation: This is the source of the ruling. Most of the time this will be Decipher.

 

Searching

You can search the rulings by using the search box.


By typing what your looking for and pressing search. You can quickly and easily find the ruling you need. This example shows a person looking for "force drain".



The Rulings Minder will highlight every where your text is found. Here you can see what it looks like.



The Rulings Minder only allows simple searches. Do not use "+ or and" in your searches.

Tip: By typing "ALL" in the search box you can get a listing of all the current rulings. Warning: this might be a large list.

Jump To

You can Jump to the ruling your looking for by using the jump to box. The jump box contains the titles of all the rulings. When you click jump to, the Rulings Minder returns all the rulings that have anything to do with your jump to selection.


Rulings Check Up

The Rulings Minder 'tracks' the date all of the rulings are added. If you have cookies enabled on your browser, it will tell you which rulings have been added since you last checked. This is useful if all you want is to be up-to-date on all the current rulings. To have the Rulings Minder perform a check up click on check up at the top of the page.



Printer Friendly

You can get a complete printer friendly copy of the current rulings to take with you to tournaments. Just click on the print link at the top of the page and have the new browser window print out the current rulings.
