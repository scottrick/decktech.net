What the Heck is going on here?

Welcome to our controversy / rumors page. This is where everything really hits the fan! People can post any rumors related to swccg they have heard or sometimes even dreamed up here. Trust me, if you believe everything on this page, then you are truly a fool. However, mixed in with all that will be some truly interesting information. How can you tell... you must search your feelings. Some may make you happy, some may make you angry, and some you couldn't care less about. However, we expect most people will have a strong feeling one way or another. Do you love Decipher? Do you hate Decipher? How about Ozymadius? Believe me these topics will be mixed in here and your true feelings WILL be revealed.

Use this message board similar to the links page. You may post a new topic or read / reply to topics. Frequently, we here at DeckTech will post an opened ended rumor to get everyone going. Totally stupid posts like "The Jedi Academy is gay" will be removed. You may think this is censorship, well it is. We are censoring stupidity from the world but not controversy, so don't be afraid to say what's on your mind.

Enjoy and use powers of observation, deduction and wit, and you will find this a truly rewarding experience.
