This Month's Drawing Prize:

A Justin "Juz" Pakes signed Luke Skywalker, Jedi Knight

For those who follow Star Wars, CCG, Justin "Juz" Pakes is almost legendary. Originally a Squadron Member from the great continent of Australia, Juz signed on with Decipher, Inc., the makers of Star Wars CCG to become their rules expert: A Jedi Knight. Quickly codifying the rules and clarifying the timing with the first reprint of the Glossary, Juz has consistantly worked to bring out about quick, general and fair rules to a very complex game. The consistency this and countless updates have brought, have immeasurably improved and enhanced an already great game.

In addition, Juz is also well known to the community for the many champion level tournaments he judges. A fixture at the annual World Championships, many of the players also have had the opportunity to meet Juz at various opens to which he travels . These countless hours of work and travel have validly earned him the nickname "The hardest working man in Star Wars CCG".

On a personal side Juz's charisma, dry humor, friendliness, talent and perhaps most importantly, outspoken/no nonsense attitude makes him a favorite of players, and especially here at Decktech where such qualities are greatly appreciated.

Juz, of course, works for Decipher, Inc., the maker of many fine trading card games including Star Wars CCG , Star Trek , Jedi Knights , Young Jedi, and the new success on the block, Lord of the Rings. Visit their website (and the links above) to find out more about their games.

This card is the Ultra Rare Luke Skywalker, Jedi Knight from Death Star II. This is a highly collectible card, and will be sure to gain in value. In addition to being one of the most collectible cards, Luke Skywalker, JK is easily one of the most powerful cards in the game. This card, signed as it is, is a must for any collector, player, or fan of Star Wars CCG.
