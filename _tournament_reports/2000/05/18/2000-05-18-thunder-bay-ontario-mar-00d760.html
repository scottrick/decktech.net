Thunder Bay, Ontario is located away from anything on the shores of Lake Superior. That outta the way we have 6 regular players but do have about 10 that play SWCCG. We decided one day to run a tourney but not at all a regular style. So the 6 regulars decided we'd make it fun. We played the tourney over 2 gaming sessions and set it up as such; No deck changes were allowed once we started so we had to list our deck contents and leave the list with the store owner who could check the decks if a question arose (never happened cause we trust each other enough). So basically we had to play each person once over these 2 days and the final game was between the 1,2 3,4 5,6 players. So basically we played 6 games each 3 DS 3 LS. Now that I explained our unorthadoxed tourney setup I can get onto the regular tourney report.
