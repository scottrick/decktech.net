after im finished reasserting my dominance in the swccg universe friday night we decide to leave and go get some food...since its really late(like 11 or 12) we decide to nix the steak dinner and head to denny's...unfortunately for me the steak dinner was rescheduled for this coming friday when we head to bill's domain for the regional. anyhow...the denny's isnt exactly gay denny's but it'll have to do.

after that's taken care of we head to the gaming area and get signed up.

fw 2(+18)


game 2 light side vs chris bently isb ors scum


i get both nudjs first turn and draw up to find my grabber...excellent...i grab none shall pass the first time and that's huge...i kill gailid the turn after he drops but he gets another one so i could be in trouble...boussh comes to the ac to slow that stuff down but she gets the nevar treatment...too bad for him i drew another one. so she stays the whole game and lets me drain for icons. i set up at his jp with boshek and a bothan for awhile and we trade drains. eventually ig88 takes care of that...battle plan is limiting what he can do but its hurtin me too since i only draw 1 location the entire game. he drops some stuff on yavin but i get rid of it each time. finally i set up with obi in the rancor pit when he's got about 10-12 cards left...he comes down with 2 ors and track 5 but i smoke and they both die. scum comes out way late but it doesnt do much...it takes me a while to finish him off but after 4 or 5 turns of cat and mouse i finish him off

fw 4 (+31)


game 3 dark side vs charlie bad player herren ass's deck


i know how to beat this pile of @#$% raph calls a deck. i set up early and flip with 3 sites and come down with s+d. opp enf and grabber in opening hand stops the visored first time he plays it. he comes down with many epps early and clears me off all but one site ridding himself of s+d. i finally get kashyyyk and drop ships to drain. afa hits a couple times but once i was able to battle him. ote is out but not hurting. a monnok nets 3 afa and 2 visored and he only plays one more the entire game. vader fears fury so he stays in my hand. melas with gimer come in front of igar in boat and i search for mara...she's there and i battle but he houjix. he finally flip me back with boshek and glig1 but i wipe them out 2 turns later. drains of 2 and 1 eventually do him in...especially after i reflip...

fw 6(+43)
