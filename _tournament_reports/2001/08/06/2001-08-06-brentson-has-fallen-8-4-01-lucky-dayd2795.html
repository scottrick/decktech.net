Game 1 My RANCOURT vs McGann’s WYS Solid

So wys is a good deck to play with my rancor, becuase ppl stay on tatooine and i can eat them

more easily that way. I drop my dbay behind the Palace and wait for him to deploy to the ground,

which is a mistake b/c from now on i should try and take the cantina. Once i got the palace set up,

i just waited. Early on i stole someone, i think it was talon. He has artoo/threepio out so he drops

melas to battle 4lom (who was escorted) and i had to lose 4lom. he dbay transited back towards the

cantina and i got screwed. he beat me out of space (which sucked) but i couldnt pull enough ships

and its really not that difficult to get rid of WYS ships without weapons, even with dengar out. So

bacially he sets up huge drains. And i go after him and take out chewie and epp luke before having

to make my long and lonly walk to the audience chamber. I get to the dungeon, drop my guys off,

and drop the rancor. i end up eating luke and talon for a 30 force swing, but his drain of 6 a turn

was killing me, and i was paying to drain. in retrospect i shouldve just dropped the virago or

something to tatooine to kill battle order, but it did me in. i had four force left when he had two.

which means i can only get him down to one, instead i just dropped epp fett to screw around and he

won by 2.

0(-2)
