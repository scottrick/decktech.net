I woke up needing a shower and a haircut, I got both, found my Yankees shirt (which is a little league shirt from when I was ten, which just kicks a-s-s!), and went to the top of my hill around 10:15, Chris picked me up with Ernest already in the car. Thanks Chris. The tournement started at noon so we killed time at McDonalds where I ate my fries, Cat gave me his then and I had a stomach ache the rest of the day. We get to ralph’s and get all settled in for the tournement, to my suprise a female show’s up to the tournment, this is going to be the first time I have ever seen a woman play star wars. So yeah.

Drafting:

We got 4 packs of Tatooine and 2 packs of Jabba’s palace. I was called for dark, I got two rares out of the whole thing, Sebulba and Ephant mon. Pretty good.I also alot of characters, and drafted no pit droids cause they stunk. I guess Marco from Santa Barbara got not only a Obi-Wan but a Qui-gon also. He got 4th out of 6 people....go figure. So yeah, we built decks and here we go. Oh, BTW, we also got to play with 3 battle grounds and dark got to play with Darth w/stick and light got to play with luke w/stick.

Ernest dosen’t know it but he is a very good player, I have a very very bad memory so lemme just some up some high lights. Ghana Gleemort IS god.
