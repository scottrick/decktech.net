David Jones&#8217; Huntdown Pods vs. Mark Chambers&#8217; PodProfit.

At first, I thought David would throw down an easy win by 25 or so, but then the game got started. He tried to drop Mara with her Stick at the Audience Chamber to kill Qui-Gon and Padme. He got Rocked, and Hard. He Swung and missed Qui-gon, then drew a 0 for destiny. He lost Mara plus 6 more cards. Then more stuff happened that I can&#8217;t remember, so he won the game. Props to Dave.


Round 2: My WYS vs. Chris Compton&#8217;s Dark Deal

This Game started off pretty bad for Chris, because first turn, I drop Dash and ship to Kessel for a drain of three, and because of His icons at Cloud City, I am able to drop Melas at the Cantina, and an RFC with Dash to flip my Objective. Second turn for him and he still didn&#8217;t have the executor out, and then it got bad, as on my second turn, I drained for 5. Then, I gave Melas some Help, and then I was almost untouchable when I dropped Lando in Falcon at Kessel with Dash. He then proceeded to try to clear out my Cantina with Mara, but she got barriered. The next turn, I drop an RFC, and then gave Mara a ride on the Attrition Bus, but she made Melas hit and F=0.
In the end, It was My win.
