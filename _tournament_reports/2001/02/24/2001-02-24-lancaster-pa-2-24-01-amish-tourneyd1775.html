Game 2 my LS vs. Jason McSherry 1700 something’s MKOS abyssians

well, Jason had MAJOR problems with his MKOS deck, including no abyssians, no abby
ornaments, and several other key cards he forgot to put in, as he rushed to just throw 20
cards in at the last second, which cost him the game. he basically deploy a bunch of guys
on the sail barge, and ran around, while draining at the AC, and the lower passages. I was
harvesting like crazy, getting 8 of them off over the game (I only managed to play one
before something special planned for them hit the table). I drained for about 10-15 force
the whole game, while he drained for about 30 some, all thanks to gailid, but I played 8
harvests, and retrieved a WHOLE lot. I placed boelo and xizor out of play with OOC/TT,
and it was all good. the biggest battle of the game occurred when he had xizor, boelo, a
merc pilot, bib, snoova, and djas puhr on the sail barge, and he initiated against my lone
jedi Luke w/ saber, and freshly stole vibro axe. he retrieved his two for scum (which he
did about 3-4 time over the course of the game, very annoying), and I promptly played
fallen portal, targeting the merc, and boelo, praying for a good draw. I get a 4, and with
the +2, they are both lost, and he’s suddenly screwed. he gets a 2 for his destiny, and I
think that I draw either a 2, or a random 6, but I&#8217;m not sure which. anyway, I end up
losing Luke, with his saber and axe, and one more, or something like that. Luke never
comes back (none of the 3 in the deck :-( ) and I just drain with Ben at the farm (and his
saber too) while he drains for about 6 a turn, and I&#8217;m getting 4 back. I beat down on him
once or twice, but in the end, it was the 7 harvests that won it. we had time called, and I
had played my second to last harvest, and we counted up the cards in our decks, I had 35,
he had 19, so I get the timed win.

Timed Win: 3 (+54)

well, James is a great player, despite his rating. he&#8217;s the one that enlightened me, and

tracked two sixes for Mara to duel jedi Luke w/ saber, he had @#$%ty draws, but we tied (I

extra overkill, DOH!) so nothing happened, except he beat down on Mara jade with some
extra destiny thanks to ralltiir freighter cappys, and I sucked really bad in this game. he

well, this game was an incredible matchup. he had something special planned for him (I
played the only people who had it, just about, it sooooo figures) but it didn&#8217;t matter
